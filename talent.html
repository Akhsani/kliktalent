Band

Klavikula

  • Overview

    Story

    is an Indonesian pop band based in Yogyakarta, formed in December 2015 at Universitas Gadjah Mada by Novega Ghufron Arlinka (Guitar), Widanta Kharisma Nugraha (Keyboard), Fuad Noor Rahardyan (Drum), Ria Santi Oktaviani Manik (Vocal) and Roby Eka Pramudita (Bass). It is a new formation of the band after 3 of the member before decided to left the band in early November 2015. A month later, Novega as the founder of this band initiate to looking for the new member. From Gadjah Mada Chamber Orchestra, he met Fuad Rahardyan, Roby Eka and Ria Santi Oktaviani that was announced as the new drummer, bassist and vocalist. By April 23rd 2016, Klavikula released its new version single, “Mulai Dari Mana”. Klavikula will release their debut mini album “Unspoken” in November 4th 2017.
